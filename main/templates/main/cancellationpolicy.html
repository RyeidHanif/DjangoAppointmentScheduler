Appointment Cancellation Policy

Effective as of July 2025

1. Purpose

This cancellation policy exists to ensure reliability and respect for everyone’s time — both service providers and customers. Booking an appointment is a commitment, and last-minute cancellations or no-shows severely disrupt the system.

2. Cancellation Deadlines

  • Appointments must be cancelled at least 24 hours before the scheduled start time.
  • Any cancellation made within 12 hours of the appointment will be considered a late cancellation.
  • No-shows are treated the same as late cancellations.

3. Customer Ban Policy

To prevent abuse, the following rules apply strictly to all customers:

  • If a customer has more than 3 late cancellations or no-shows within any rolling 30-day period, their account will be automatically and permanently banned.
  • There are no warnings, no reviews — this is a zero-tolerance policy.

4. Provider Ban Policy

Service providers are held to an equal standard of responsibility:

  • If a provider cancels or no-shows more than 3 times within any rolling 30-day period, their account will be automatically banned from the platform.
  • Customers rely on provider availability — inconsistency is not tolerated.

5. Emergencies

If you missed an appointment due to a legitimate emergency, you may contact support within 24 hours with documented proof. Exceptions are rare and granted only in verifiable circumstances.

6. Final Note

Repeated cancellations waste time, harm trust, and break the scheduling system for everyone. This policy is strictly enforced with no leniency. By using our platform, you agree to these terms.

Notice: Violations exceeding allowed limits will result in an immediate permanent ban without appeal.
