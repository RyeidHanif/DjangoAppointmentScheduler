Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 5, 2025

At DjangoAppointmentScheduler, your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our services.

We may collect the following types of information:

Your information is used to:
